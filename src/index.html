Rules

Those are the rules you must follow while interacting with Furry Tech channels. Non-compliance to those terms of usage will result in your removal of the room.

General Rules

  • No NSFW (Not Safe For Work) images or topics unless the room is explicitly marked as
  • Avoid political issues, those aren't disallowed, but we all hate heated discussions
  • Do not mass invite
  • Please use an appropriate image and username when joining the room (use /myroomnick and /myroomavatar if needed)
  • Don't be rude, it is fine to have an argument with someone as long as it stays civil
  • If you have issues you can't resolve on your own, please send a direct message to a staff member
  • SFW (Safe For Work) roleplay is allowed as long as it doesn't become crazy
  • If you possess a cat, a tax is automatically applied to you, please send a picture of your cat in the channel to pay it
